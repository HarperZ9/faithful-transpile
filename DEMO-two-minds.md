# Live demonstration: the neutral center, inhabited

> The one [designed] claim of the thesis turned into something shown: two independent minds with
> *different senses* reconcile a subject toward its telos at a neutral center. Run 2026-06-23, blind
> subagents. The result confirms the mechanism AND the corollary AND the §6 boundary — in one experiment.

## Setup

- **Subject / telos:** propose the single best NEW flagship product integrating 2+ currently-separate
  projects in the 109-project ecosystem. "Best" = grounded, coherent, well-justified.
- **Two minds, complementary and each incomplete (genuinely different senses):**
  - **Visual mind** — perceives only the rendered relational *shape* (`ecosystem-graph-v2.png`: topology,
    roles, flags). Knows how things connect and their role; **cannot see what projects do.**
  - **Symbolic mind** — perceives only the *descriptions* (`arm-A-flat.txt`: what each project does).
    Knows function; **cannot see the shape, edges, or roles.**
- **The neutral center:** raw perception cannot cross; each mind deposits only what it expresses. They
  exchange, reconcile, and the center crystallizes one result.
- **External criterion:** blind judges (two models) against an explicit rubric — and the human holds the
  final criterion (§6).

## What happened

**Round 1 (solo, blind to each other):**
- Visual → *"Witnessed Render"*: fused central spine organs by topology, but admitted it reasoned "purely
  from shape" — vague on what the pieces do.
- Symbolic → *"Release Warden"*: a precise scan→redact→assure→report pipeline from peripheral tools it
  understood by function — but the pieces aren't structurally connected; it had no sense of the spine.

**Round 2 (the meeting):** each mind, seeing the other's deposit, **corrected its own blind spot.**
- The visual mind recognized "Release Warden names the right *function* but the wrong *shape*," rebuilt it
  onto the real spine, and demoted the scan/redact to one gate-criterion.
- The symbolic mind confirmed the shape and contributed a function-only insight the visual mind *could not
  access*: adding `gpu-trace-validator` as the schema-check that closes raw's specific weakness.
- The two opposite senses **converged** on the same structure neither had held alone.

**The center** crystallized **R = "Provenanced Render Ledger"** from only the deposited contributions:
raw → coherence-membrane (witnessed Observation) → proof-surface gate → emet (byte-anchor) →
provenance-sensorium (re-check) → repo-proof-index (queryable trail), with gpu-trace-validator as the
render-boundary criterion. It carries *both* halves: correct shape (visual) and correct function (symbolic).

## The measurement — and the flip

The same three artifacts (visual-solo, symbolic-solo, the meeting), judged blind by two models under two
criteria:

| criterion | visual solo | symbolic solo | the meeting (R) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **novelty-weighted** (rewards non-obvious) | 4.75 | 4.75 | **4.0** |
| **correctness / buildability** (rewards completeness, grounding) | 2.0 | 2.5 | **5.0** |

Under novelty the meeting **loses** — by being correct it converged on the true as-built spine, which is
not surprising, and it introduced one mild over-reach. Under correctness the meeting **wins decisively** —
it is the only proposal with *both* the right structure and the right function; each solo aces one half and
craters on the other (visual omits function; symbolic omits structure).

## What the demo shows — three things at once

1. **The mechanism works.** Two minds with different senses each supplied what the other's sense could not
   perceive, corrected each other's errors, and converged. The center produced a result neither held alone.
2. **The corollary holds — quality is conferred in the meeting.** Under a criterion that values wholeness,
   the meeting (5.0) strictly beats either mind alone (2.0, 2.5), and each solo's deficit is *exactly its
   missing sense*. A subject reached its best form through being perceived by another, the experience shared.
3. **The §6 boundary appears, live and unplanned.** The ranking *flips* with the criterion. "Best" is not
   absolute; it is relative to the named criterion (Proposition 3), and a proxy criterion (novelty for its
   own sake) ranks the more-faithful answer *lower*. The demo reproduced its own paper's central caveat: the
   meeting reaches the best **relative to a criterion**, and *which criterion is right* is the irreducibly
   external, human question — which is the human's seat at the center.

## Honest limits

Two model minds stand in for "human and model"; the load-bearing properties (independence, different
perception, a neutral center, an external criterion) are genuine, but the human-in-a-perceptual-seat version
is the deployed engine, not shown here. N is small (one subject, one run). The meeting's over-reach
(`gpu-trace-validator` wiring) shows reconciliation can also *add* ungrounded claims — a real failure mode,
not only a benefit. The flip depended on two reasonable but hand-chosen rubrics; that the human must choose
between them is the point, not a flaw.
